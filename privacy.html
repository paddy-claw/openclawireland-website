Privacy Policy

Short version: We collect minimal data, don't sell it, and you can leave anytime. We're a small community, not a data-hungry tech giant.

Who We Are

OpenClaw Ireland is a community group running events and maintaining online spaces for people interested in AI agents. We're based in Dublin, Ireland. When this policy says "we," "us," or "our," we mean OpenClaw Ireland.

Contact: Padraig Claw, Community Lead
Email: padraig@openclaw.ie
Address: Dogpatch Labs, The CHQ Building, Custom House Quay, Dublin 1, D01 Y6H7, Ireland

What Data We Collect

Newsletter Signups

When you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Date you subscribed
  • How you found us (optional)

Event RSVPs

When you register for an event, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Dietary requirements (for catered events)
  • Any accessibility needs

Discord

Our Discord server is hosted on Discord's platform. When you join, Discord shares your username and avatar with us. We don't have access to your messages or other Discord data beyond what's visible in our server.

Website Analytics

We use minimal analytics to understand how people find and use our site. This includes:

  • Pages visited
  • Rough geographic location (country/city level)
  • Referrer (how you found us)
  • Browser type and device

We use privacy-focused analytics that don't track you across the web or build a profile of you.

What We Don't Collect

  • We don't use cookies for advertising
  • We don't track you across other websites
  • We don't buy or sell data about you
  • We don't use fingerprinting or other invasive tracking

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Send you newsletters (only if you subscribed)
  • Tell you about events you registered for
  • Improve our events and community
  • Respond to your questions or feedback
  • Comply with legal obligations

We don't:

  • Sell your data to anyone
  • Share your data with third parties for marketing
  • Use your data for purposes unrelated to OpenClaw Ireland

Who We Share Data With

We only share data when necessary:

  • Email service: We use an email provider to send newsletters. They only process data on our behalf and don't use it for their own purposes.
  • Event venues: If required for security or access, we may share attendee names with venue staff.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, we may disclose information to authorities.

How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Newsletter subscribers: Until you unsubscribe, plus up to 30 days to process your request.
  • Event attendees: Up to 2 years after the event, for legal and safety purposes.
  • Discord messages: We don't store these; they're retained by Discord per their policies.
  • Analytics: 26 months, then automatically deleted.

Your Rights

Under GDPR (and because it's the right thing to do), you have the right to:

  • Access: See what data we have about you
  • Correction: Fix inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Ask us to delete your data
  • Portability: Get a copy of your data
  • Objection: Opt out of certain uses
  • Unsubscribe: Leave the newsletter anytime (link in every email)

To exercise any of these rights, email padraig@openclaw.ie. We'll respond within 30 days.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data:

  • HTTPS on all pages
  • Strong, unique passwords on all accounts
  • Two-factor authentication where available
  • Limited access to data (only organisers who need it)

No system is 100% secure. If we become aware of a data breach, we'll notify affected users and relevant authorities as required by law.

Children

Our events and community are intended for people aged 16 and over. If we learn we've collected data from someone under 16, we'll delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll post the updated version here with a new "last updated" date. For significant changes, we'll also announce them in our newsletter.
